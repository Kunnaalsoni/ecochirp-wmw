Waste Management Bootcamp (WMB) is 45 days virtual Bootcamp focused on providing a rigorous learning experience that nurtures future leaders for the space of waste management. The mission of WMB is to serve as a launchpad for building skills of youth who are aspiring leaders, sustainability, and/or environmental enthusiasts and want to build a career in this sector or are already on their journey but are looking to upskill themselves.

Your experience & learnings at WMB will enable you to become a strong, highly motivated & skilled individual, with a mission to solve the widespread issue of waste management across the nation.

Learn from the nations top experts, academicians, researchers, policymakers, politicians, and bureaucrats from their experiences.

Build with the help of experts & mentors as part of your experiential learning module

Act upon your own built solution along with your team & under the guidance of ecochirp experts.
